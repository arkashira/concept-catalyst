# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Concept Catalyst empowers indie hackers and creators to effortlessly generate and validate software ideas, breaking through the ideation bottleneck with AI-driven insights."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**
1. "Transform your creative process with Concept Catalyst, the AI tool that turns your ideas into validated software concepts in minutes."
2. "Unlock your potential as an indie hacker with Concept Catalyst, the AI-powered platform designed to streamline your ideation and validation journey."
3. "Say goodbye to creative blocks; Concept Catalyst uses AI to help you discover and validate your next big software idea."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Alex the Indie Hacker**
   - **Daily Job:** Building and launching MVPs for various software projects.
   - **Budget:** $50/month for tools that enhance productivity and creativity.

2. **Persona 2: Jamie the Tech Creator**
   - **Daily Job:** Developing content and software solutions for niche markets.
   - **Budget:** $75/month for tools that assist in idea generation and market validation.

3. **Persona 3: Sam the Startup Founder**
   - **Daily Job:** Leading a small team to create innovative software products.
   - **Budget:** $100/month for tools that streamline the ideation and development process.

## Channels
1. **Content Marketing (Blog/SEO)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $20 per customer acquired through organic traffic and lead magnets.

2. **Social Media Advertising (Facebook/Instagram)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 per customer acquired through targeted ads focused on indie hackers and creators.

3. **Partnerships with Indie Hacker Communities**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $30 per customer acquired through collaborations and promotions within established communities.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing Concept Catalyst + social media teasers.
- **Week 2:** Publish case studies of successful indie hackers using AI for ideation.
- **Week 3:** Host a webinar on overcoming ideation challenges with AI.
- **Week 4:** Release an eBook on the ideation process for indie hackers.
- **Week 5:** Share user testimonials and success stories on social media.
- **Week 6:** Create an infographic summarizing the benefits of AI in software ideation.
- **Week 7:** Publish a blog post on common pitfalls in the ideation process and how to avoid them.
- **Week 8:** Launch a video series featuring interviews with successful indie hackers.
- **Week 9:** Conduct a survey on ideation challenges and share findings.
- **Week 10:** Release a podcast episode discussing the future of AI in software development.
- **Week 11:** Share behind-the-scenes content on the development of Concept Catalyst.
- **Week 12:** Recap the journey of Concept Catalyst and announce upcoming features.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and user interface; begin beta testing with selected users.
- **D-0:** Official launch of Concept Catalyst; execute marketing campaigns across chosen channels.
- **D+30:** Gather user feedback and iterate on product features; start collecting success metrics.
- **D+90:** Analyze user engagement and retention; set goals for future product enhancements based on user data.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Target of 500 DAUs by D+90.
- **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) Target:** $10,000 by D+90.
