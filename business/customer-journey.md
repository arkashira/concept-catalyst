# Customer Journey Map for Concept-Catalyst

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Indie hackers and creators experience frustration with the ideation process, leading to a search for solutions.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about AI tools that can assist in idea generation; overwhelming number of resources available.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, curiosity.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide engaging content (blogs, videos) that clearly explain how Concept-Catalyst can alleviate ideation challenges.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and content engagement rates.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Users actively seek solutions and come across Concept-Catalyst through marketing efforts or word-of-mouth.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about the effectiveness of AI-generated ideas; skepticism about the product's ability to validate concepts.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful yet cautious, intrigued.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer free trials or demos that showcase the product's capabilities; share testimonials and case studies from early adopters.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from visits to sign-ups for trials or demos.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users sign up for a trial or demo of Concept-Catalyst.
- **Friction Points**: Initial learning curve; potential technical issues during setup or usage.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety; eagerness to see results.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide comprehensive onboarding support, including tutorials and responsive customer service; offer personalized guidance based on user goals.
- **Metric**: User engagement metrics during the trial period (e.g., number of ideas generated, time spent using the product).

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users successfully generate and validate ideas using Concept-Catalyst, leading to a decision to purchase.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about ongoing costs; potential comparison with other tools.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, relief, confidence.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer flexible pricing plans and loyalty rewards; provide ongoing support and resources to help users maximize their use of the product.
- **Metric**: Rate of conversion from trial to paid subscriptions.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users experience success with the product and seek to explore additional features or services.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about advanced features; potential plateau in idea generation.
- **User Emotions**: Ambition, desire for growth, curiosity.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Regularly update users on new features and improvements; create a community platform for users to share experiences and ideas.
- **Metric**: User retention rate and upsell conversion rate for additional features or services.
