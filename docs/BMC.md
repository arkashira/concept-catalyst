# Business Model Canvas – **concept‑catalyst**

| **Building Block** | **Details** |
|--------------------|-------------|
| **Value Proposition** | • **Instant, AI‑driven software idea generation** – creators receive a list of concrete, market‑validated product concepts in seconds.<br>• **Built‑in validation** – each idea is scored against real‑world demand signals (search trends, Reddit/Indie‑Hackers chatter, early‑buyer intent) and a willingness‑to‑pay estimate.<br>• **Idea‑to‑MVP roadmap** – the AI also suggests a minimal feature set, tech stack, and rough effort estimate, turning a vague notion into an actionable plan.<br>• **Risk‑free trial** – first 5 ideas are free; users only pay when they decide to pursue a concept. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Indie hackers & solo founders** – looking for the next product to build.<br>2. **Creator‑entrepreneurs** (YouTubers, podcasters, educators) who monetize digital tools.<br>3. **Early‑stage startups** that need a pipeline of vetted concepts for internal hackathons.<br>4. **Product‑design agencies** that want to pitch fresh concepts to clients.<br>5. **Enterprise innovation teams** seeking rapid ideation for internal tooling. |
| **Channels** | • **Web SaaS platform** – primary UI (React + Next.js) hosted on Vercel/Netlify.<br>• **REST & GraphQL API** – allows integration into IDE plugins, no‑code builders, and community bots.<br>• **Marketplace listings** – Product Hunt, Indie Hackers, Hacker News, and the Axentx “Idea Exchange”.<br>• **Content & community** – weekly newsletter, webinars, and a Discord/Slack community for feedback loops.<br>• **Partner integrations** – Zapier, Notion, and Airtable connectors for idea export. |
| **Customer Relationships** | • **Self‑service onboarding** – guided walkthrough, AI‑driven tutorial, and in‑app help.<br>• **Community‑driven support** – public Q&A, idea‑rating leaderboards, and peer feedback.<br>• **Dedicated success managers** for enterprise & agency accounts.<br>• **Feedback loop** – every generated idea is stored in the Axentx BRAIN; user actions (accept, reject, iterate) continuously improve the model. |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Subscription tiers** (monthly/annual):<br>   - *Free*: 5 ideas/month, no validation scores.<br>   - *Pro* ($29/mo): 50 ideas, full validation, API access (10 k calls).<br>   - *Team* ($99/mo): unlimited ideas, team collaboration, custom validation filters.<br>2. **Pay‑per‑validation** – $0.05 per validated idea for occasional users.<br>3. **Marketplace commission** – 10 % fee on sales of MVPs built from catalyst ideas (via Axentx partner marketplace).<br>4. **Enterprise licensing** – white‑label deployment, SSO, on‑prem GPU clusters, custom data sources. |
| **Key Resources** | • **AI models** – vLLM inference engine + SGLang structured generation, fine‑tuned on Axentx’s 22 M+ auto‑pairs, 7 M instr‑resp, 6 M messages, 1.7 M query‑resp datasets.<br>• **Axentx BRAIN (pgvector)** – vector store for idea embeddings, market‑signal vectors, and validation metrics.<br>• **Compute infrastructure** – GPU clusters (NVIDIA A100/A40) on AWS/GCP, auto‑scaling via Kubernetes.<br>• **Product team** – PM, senior ML engineer, full‑stack dev, QA, reviewer (hard‑gate validation).<br>• **IP & licenses** – open‑source frameworks (vLLM, SGLang) plus proprietary validation pipelines. |
| **Key Activities** | 1. **Model training & continual fine‑tuning** – ingest new market data (search trends, Reddit, Twitter) weekly.<br>2. **Idea generation pipeline** – prompt engineering, structured output via SGLang, ranking with validation scores.<br>3. **Validation engine** – cross‑reference generated ideas with demand signals, compute willingness‑to‑pay (WTP) using regression on historic conversion data.<br>4. **Product delivery** – UI/UX design, API development, documentation, and CI/CD (GitHub Actions).<br>5. **Quality hard‑gate** – reviewer team validates a random 5 % sample for bias, feasibility, and compliance before release.<br>6. **Community & growth** – content marketing, partnership outreach, and A/B testing of pricing. |
